Our Story & Vision

Healing River Ministries was founded on a simple promise: that God's grace is a river that never runs dry. We are a community of believers dedicated to seeing lives transformed through the power of the Holy Spirit.

Our Mission

To lead people into a growing relationship with Jesus Christ by creating an environment where healing is expected and restoration is celebrated.

What We Believe

We believe the scriptures are the inspired word of God, that community makes life better, and that God still restores the weary today.
